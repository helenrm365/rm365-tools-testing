How to Use

1

Select Employee

Choose the employee from the dropdown menu

2

Choose Finger

Click on a finger in the hand diagram to select it

3

Scan Fingerprint

Place finger on scanner (scanning starts automatically)

4

Verify & Save

Check the preview, then click "Save Fingerprint" to complete

Note: Requires the Local Hardware Bridge service running on this machine. Biometric data is processed locally for security.
Technical Details
Format: ANSI-378
Interface: Local Bridge (Port 8080)
Timeout: 10 seconds
